📖 GCE Registration Guide

🎯 Welcome to NOERMS — Your Digital GCE Registration Platform

This system replaces the traditional paper-based Form G3. Follow the 4 steps below to complete your registration for the 2024 GCE Examinations.

📝
Step 1 — Form G3
Fill in your personal information, school details, and choose your examination series (O Level or A Level).
📚
Step 2 — Subject Selection
Choose your examination subjects. O Level candidates select up to 8 subjects. A Level candidates select 3–4 subjects based on their series.
📎
Step 3 — Upload Documents
Upload required documents: Birth Certificate, Passport Photo, Previous results (if applicable), and School Confirmation Letter.
💳
Step 4 — Payment
Pay the registration fee of 25,000 FCFA via Mobile Money (MTN/Orange) or Bank Transfer. Keep your transaction reference.

📋 Required Documents

1
Birth Certificate — Official birth certificate or certified copy. Must be legible. (PDF or JPG, max 2MB)
2
Passport Photo — Recent colour passport-size photograph on white background. (JPG, max 500KB)
3
Previous Examination Results — Required for A Level candidates only. O Level result slip. (PDF or JPG)
4
School Confirmation Letter — Official letter from your school confirming candidacy. Signed and stamped by the Principal.
